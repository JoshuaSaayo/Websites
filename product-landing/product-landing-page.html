Honkai: Star Rail Top up

  • First Top-Up Bonus

    If your in-game Character has never topped up via the game or any platform, you can:
    Top up 60 Oneiric Shard to get 120 Oneiric Shard;
    Top up 300+30 Oneiric Shard to get 600 Oneiric Shard;
    Top up 980+110 Oneiric Shard to get 1,960 Oneiric Shard;
    Top up 1,980+260 Oneiric Shard to get 3,960 Oneiric Shard;
    Top up 3,280+600 Oneiric Shard to get 6,560 Oneiric Shard;
    Top up 6,480+1,600 Oneiric Shard to get 12,960 Oneiric Shard.

  • Express Supply Pass

    1. Purchasing the Express Supply Pass will immediately grant you 300 Oneiric Shards and extend the duration of the Pass by 30 days.
    2. While the Express Supply Pass is active, you will receive 90 Stellar Jades every day upon logging into the game. This reward is reset at 4:00 AM server time every day. Stellar Jades that have not been claimed due to login inactivity will not be refunded.

  • Attention

    1. You may only purchase more Express Supply Passes if your current Pass is valid for less than 179 days.
    2. You will be unable to purchase Express Supply Passes if your current Pass is valid for 179 days or more. Should you obtain new Passes while having a Pass that is valid for 179 days or more, the duration of your current Pass will not be extended and 330 Oneiric Shards will be refunded to your game account.
